Advanced U-Net for Multi-class Segmentation of Mammography
Published Wed 06 September 2023
Non-contact vital-sign estimation allows the monitoring of physiological parameters (such as heart rate, respiratory rate, and peripheral oxygen saturation) without contact electrodes or sensors. The goal of our group is to assess the vital signs of newborns using the latest artificial intelligence techniques. Further, we expect to develop new algorithms to mitigate the effects of light and darkness on vital sign extraction. Ultimately, we expect to develop a single unified model that simultaneously monitor heart rate, respiratory rate, and peripheral oxygen saturation in newborns.
